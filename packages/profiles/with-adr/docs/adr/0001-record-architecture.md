# ADR-0001: Record architecture decisions

**Date:** 2025-10-31
**Status:** Accepted
**Deciders:** KB Labs Team
**Last Reviewed:** 2025-11-03
**Tags:** [architecture, process]

## Context

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) provide a way to document important architectural decisions in the project.

## Decision

Record architecture decisions using ADR format.

## Consequences

**Positive:**

- Provides historical record of decisions
- Enables better understanding of why decisions were made
- Facilitates knowledge sharing across team

**Negative:**

- Requires discipline to maintain and update ADRs
- Additional documentation overhead
